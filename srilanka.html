Colombo & Sightseeing

An adventurous city

A bright smiling sun all year round, a rich history of past & present, Colombo is an exotic island with marvelous discoveries and an authentic culture.

Colombo, rich in diverse cultures and a growing cosmopolitan city has a lot to offer and is dotted with numerous interesting and important tourist attractions including colonial era buildings.

Be it an adventurous journey, a relaxing massage or a wellness holiday, there is excitement filled wherever you go. Indulge in the rich tantalizing spices, take a sip of Ceylon tea and make some magical memories on the beautiful island we call home.

Here is a list of shops, restaurants and saloons that will help you during your stay here in this tropical island.
