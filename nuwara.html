Places to explore in
Nuwara Eliya


St.Clair’s Fall
 Beautiful St. Clair’s Fall located on the Kotmale river will be truly an exception view for many of you as itss one of the widest falls in the island.

Gregory Lake
One of the main places to visit in Nuwara Eliya was created by the British Governer Sir William Gregory in 1873. Gregory Lake is one of the ideal location to have a walk and relax near the river banks. If an individual pleases to stay with nature, they could visit the park by the lake or they could engage with other activities in the lake such as a boat ride

Devon Falls
Devon Falls is a beautiful Waterfall, that is 97 meters high and ranked 19th highest in the Island. The fall is one of the main attractions in Nuwara Eliya

Hakgala Botanical Garden
Hakgala Botanical Garden is one of the five botanical gardens in Sri Lanka and one of the main attractions in Nuwara Eliya. Hakgala Botanical Garden is situated on the Nuwara Eliya-Badulla main road, 16 km from Nuwara Eliya.

Horton Plains
 The plains’ vegetation is grasslands interspersed with montane forest and includes many endemic woody plants. Large herds of Sri Lankan sambar deer feature as typical mammals and the park is also has an important bird Area with many species not only endemic to Sri Lanka but also restricted to the Horton Plains.
